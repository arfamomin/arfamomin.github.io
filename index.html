Here are some technical projects I've worked on.

  • World Energy Mix Visualizer, built in August 2023 with D3 Start to Finish. Use this web tool to explore how each country sources energy. Data from World Bank.
  • SecureAI, built in July 2023 at the CalHacks AI with my three teammates, is an AI-powered application analyzes images to detect signs of human trafficking.
